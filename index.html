Password Safety Tips

  • Use a Secure Password Manager (Offline Is Better) - Choose a Strong Master Password.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
  • Secure Your Devices - Strong Phone Password (Avoid using FaceID or biometric authentication methods that may be employed without your consent or against your will.).
  • Do Not Use Maccas Wifi lol (Any Free and Open Network Does not Encrypted traffic).

Network Safety Tips

  • Enable Encryption (WPA2 or WPA3 encryption).
  • Change Default SSID And Password (Network/Wifi Name & Password).
  • Enable Firewalls.
  • Use Secure Protocols (HTTPS etc).
  • Ensure secure internet browsing by using blockers or anti-ad and pop-up add-ons to be safe in your online interactions.

Best Password Managers

KeePass (Offline)

I don't personally endorse widely used options like Dashlane, 1Password, NordPass, or Bitwarden. While they offer convenience for moving passwords easily, their popularity makes them attractive targets for hackers, and entrusting all your passwords online carries risks.

Best VPNs

I like Mullvad VPN, but the reality is that many individuals don't actually require a VPN; some are drawn to it merely for its perceived cool factor.

I don't personally endorse widely used options like NordVPN & Surfshark but I'm sure they are fine for your Netflix shows you want to watch in USA.

Best Web Browsers

For the majority, using Brave (similar to Chrome) or Firefox, along with uBlock and Privacy Badger extensions, and tweaking some settings, provides a secure way to stop some trackers and pop-ups. This is notably safer than standard Chrome or Edge.

Best Anti-Virus

Honestly, nowadays, many people don't require antivirus software. Most antivirus programs focus on prevention or rely on a database of known viruses, making it challenging to detect new ones. I'd only suggest using antivirus on Windows, not Mac (OSX) or Linux. BUT exercising caution when downloading, including pirated games or music, is crucial, as malicious content, like Remote Access Trojans (RATs), can even be concealed in PDFs or images.
